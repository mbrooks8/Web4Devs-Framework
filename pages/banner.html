A component that extends the entire viewport to showcase shitty marketing messages on your site.


There are four standard types of banners:

  • Default Banners: A big box for displaying things at the top of the page
  • Small Banners: A slightly smaller box for displaying things at the top of the page
  • Image Banners: A big box for calling extra attention to some special content or information.
  • Shitty Add Banner: A shitty box for shit content that noone wants to actually see.

Banners are pretty good but don't over use them.

Choosing Banner Style

Choosing a banner style depends the location that information is needed, the function for the banner, or the layout of the content is around it.


Location: Is the banner located at the top of the page or surrounded by text?


Function: Does the banner need to link to other pages or does it only need to have text that draws the readers eyes?


Layout: Try to limit the number of banners used on the webpage, but if there is a large amount of content, banners are nice to seperate large groups in single page websites and shitty banners can fill in empty space.
